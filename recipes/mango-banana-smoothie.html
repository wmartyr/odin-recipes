Mango-Banana Smoothie

Description

Start your day with a flavorful fruit smoothie. This drink is as delicious as breakfast, but also serves well as an afternoon snack or even a frosty dessert.

Ingredients

  • 1 banana
  • 1/2 cup frozen mango pieces
  • 1/3 plain yogurt
  • 1/2 cup orange-mango juice blend

Steps

  1. Combine the banana, mango, yogurt, and juice in a blender; blend until nearly smooth.
